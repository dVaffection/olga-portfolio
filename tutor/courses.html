Grammar

We cover all grammar aspects through communicative and interactive language-learning activities.

Vocabulary

Vocabulary practice is integrated into reading, writing, and speaking activities and will help you to develop your language level.

pronunciation

With our efficient accent reduction exercises you will learn to produce the standard American accent.

Listening

Learn strategies for listening for important information and understanding the main points to improve your comprehension.

Speaking

Practice conversation in a friendly, comfortable environment. We will focus on problem areas, and on fluency in speaking.

Reading

Develop your reading according to your interests. Practise writing paragraphs, letters, applications, and essays.
