About Us

We are educated and experienced English language teachers for all ages

Tutor is a multi-page HTML5 responsive site template with a unique look and feel that is suitable for any educational activities such as courses, workshops or private lessons.

The template is based on Bootstrap 3 and comes with powerful royalty free images. It can be used as a one-pager for simple website or with additional pages that are included for more complex approach.

Tutor supports parallax and counter up effects that make your site look modern. It also comes with over 400 icons from font awesome pack, fully integrated Google maps, working contact form and share links. HTML /CSS are validated and code is clean and easy to modify.

Questions and Answers

Answers to most common questions

  • How much do the classes cost?

    Classes cost $20 per 1.5 hour for one or two students. Or $100 per month if you stydy in a group.

  • Is there a minimum class time?

    The minimun class time is 1.5 hour or 2 hours. Sorry, there is no classes of one hour only.

  • Where will the classes be held?

    The classes can take place at our office or at your place. If the distance is too far to travel it is possible to find a convinient place half-way.

  • Can I cancel a class?

    Please give 24 hours notice to cancel an individual class. Group classes can not be canceled and you will be charged if you miss the class.

  • Are there any age restrictions?

    No, it is never too early or too late to learn a language. We have different programs designed for different ages.

  • How long does it take to complete a course?

    This depends on the student, on the level you have when you start the course and the time you are willing to devote to improving your language.

  • What is better individual or group classes?

    There is no right answer for this question. Generaly people chose individual classes when they have limited time available or want the teacher to be focused 100% on their needs.

  • Do I need to buy any books?

    All necessary material and copies will be provided to you at no additional charge.
