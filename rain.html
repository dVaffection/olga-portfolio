rain magazine

Logo Design.
Layout and Typography.

Rain Magazine is a Vancouver's conceptual edition about young local artists working in music, fine art and fashion. Logotype is crafted to match clean urban design of the magazine with simple but contemporary style. Big headlines and bold red colour make this edition stand out and catch readers' attention. This magazine will be a good choice for young people interested in subcultures and loving to discover alternative cultural events, music festivals and fashion shows.
A good design abides rules and principles like layout, order and symmetry. But sometimes we breake the rules a bit by using elements like variety, tension or contrast to create places for a viewer's eye.
