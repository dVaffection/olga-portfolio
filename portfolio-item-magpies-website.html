Magpie's

Responsive website design

Magpie’s believes that jewellery is a very important piece of any outfit that makes it different, unique and adds zest to the casual wardrobe. Handmade jewellery is for colourful women and this is a great way to express their personality and artistic flair.

Problem:
Magpie’s wants to build a strong community of people who love a jewellery making craft and appreciate handmade accessories.

solution:
A responsive website will help to bring business awareness and to promote the brand’s idea. It will help customers to keep track on new events, stay in touch and also to create a strong portfolio of their handmade jewellery.

user research:
Inga is a 18–25 years old school teacher. She loves nature, literature, travelling. She loves to look modern and fashionable, she believes that every day is a journey.

Responsive Design

Being responsive is being modern and mobile. The Magpie’s website is easy to access from any device what is customer friendly and very important in building a strong community.
