rent column

Logo Design and Brand Identity.
Web Design. Advertising.

Rent Column is a web based project that offers stuff for rent. Emphasizing the inexpensiveness of the idea, new design approach was crafted in order to directly communicate with the audience. The mobile-friendly web-site is part of a brand positionning project that aimed to broaden the brand's target audience. Greenish swatch with a vibrant contrast of orange gives the feeling of hospitality and is friendly for young people. Overall the new design is clean and elegant and meets perfectly the needs of the contemporary market.

web-site

New web-site design is clean and easy to navigate that allows customers to get quick access to the information they are looking for. The icons have modern look with vintage influence in order to highlight the main idea of the project.

Logotype was inspired by the shape and proportions of coins to encourage the idea of saving money. Green and orange brand colours add vibrancy and modern look to the project. They are freindly to young people who is the target audience.
Minimalist posters with bright orange background catch the people's attention and encourage them to search the web-site and the service it provides. Those posters have been designed as a part of marketing campaign to inform people about the project and to position it on the market.
I design solutions that balance strategy and style, modern trends and traditional approach.
