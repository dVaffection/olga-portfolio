banned but existed

Illustrations.
Conceptualisation. Art Direction.

The exhibition Banned but existed brings you back to Soviet time to show the way American culture was penetrating into the close Soviet society and the hidden side of its existence. A set of posters have been crafted to promote this exhibition. The concept is based on a mashup of iconic American heroes with the Russian folk ones to reflect secret but nonetheless recognizable coexistence of both cultures. The layout refers to propaganda posters of Soviet time as theoretically all Americana was banned to be substituted by its Soviet equivalents. This exhibition will be interesting for people born in USSR to bring them kind of nostalgia but also for North Americans who would like to discover the way their culture existed in a completely different society.
I want my work to be creative and I tend to generate strong concepts and creative solutions that communicate immidiatly, evoke a smile, stimulate thinking.
