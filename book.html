12 chairs

Illustration. Handlettering.

12 chairs is a classic satirical novel by Odessan Soviet authors Ilf and Petrov. This book cover was designed for 85th anniversary of the novel to pay homage to its history and immortal heritage it left for next generations. Vintage pattern and decorations bring us to that Soviet time when the story was written while modern style of the front cover illustration highlights the immortality and popularity of the story.
