Urban Boxing

Logo & Brand Identity, Print Ads, Photography

Urban Boxing is a boxing space that offers for its members a 24 hours access and a variety of boxing bags as well as all necessary work-out equipment. Urban Boxing positions itself as a community where people learn boxing through communication and experience. Members are welcome to come in any time they want to practice boxing, to train with others and to learn from others.

Urban Boxing positions itself as a club that doesn't impose you boxercising but a place where you learn boxing and build your confidence to be ready for sparrings.
