brie cheese

Logo Design. Brand Identity. Package Design.
Advertising.

The conceptual company Ferme de Brie is a French small manufacture that produces Brie cheese using authentic recipes in order to preserve the true creamy taste. The design combines retro and modern elements to emphasize the popularity of this cheese trough years. This brand can be popular among people who take care of what they eat and love food cooked as grandma did.
The copy on the packaging is a popular saying among people living in regions where Brie chesese is produced. This phrase highlights rustic origins of the product. The handlettered typography and vintage colours pay homage to old traditions.
Minimalist advertisement posters remind in a funny way that Brie cheese can be a good choice for breakfast, lunch or for a wine party. Tactile design reinforces the brand's idea of human craftsmanship.
I put care and feelings in your brand, so you will be able to connect with your customers at psychological and emotional level.
