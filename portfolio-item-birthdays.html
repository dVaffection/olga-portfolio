Birthdays

Android app UI/UX, Illustrations

You don't have to remember all your friends and relatives birthdays anymore! Keep them in a dedicated app and get a notification when a birthday is coming. The app also allows to take notes of gift ideas that come to your mind during the whole year.

  • – Have all birthdays in one place
  • – Import your Google contacts
  • – Write down gift ideas and take other notes
  • – Get a reminder of an upcoming birthday

The calendar view is a quick and easy way to see upcoming birthdays, and your birthday presents ideas or party plans. Each month features an illustration that adds some visual interest to the layout and also behaves as an anchor point while users quickly scroll through the year.

Browse through all your contacts organized alphabetically, add new people using floating action button, provide a year when a person was born, take any notes you want.

Product icon

After some research had been done, a cupcake idea was chosen for the app launch icon to visually express the product. The icon is crafted in accordance to material design rules to represent the tactile and physical quality of paper. The icon is simple, bold, and friendly, and communicates the core product idea through concept and execution.

The color palette comprises primary (purple) and accent (orange) colours. Purple is a very impressive, stylish, and romantic. This colour suggests reflective moodiness and mystery what is often associated with a birthday day. Orange is festive. It represents joy, cheerfulness, excitement and energy.

Illustrations

Bold, bright and colourful illustrations not only bring visual interest and delight to the layout but also differentiate the product and engage the user. This dynamic and content-relevant images help users to find the right month at a glance while they are quickly scrolling the calendar. They enhance the user experience and bring the app to life!
