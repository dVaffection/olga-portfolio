sugarrays

Logo Design. Brand Identity.
Web Design. Advertising.

Sugarrays is a boxing gym located in Downtown Vancouver. It offers a big variety of boxing bags as well as all necessary work-out equipment. Sugarrays positions itself as a community of boxing lovers where people learn this sport through communication and experience. This idea is reflected in a new logo representing high-five as a symbol of good work and success. The entire re-brand with bright colours will modernize their image and catch attention of new customers. The new design has sharp male character but is elegant and hits it on point.
As a part of the advertising campaign a mobile application has been developed. The purpose of this application is to involve customers into boxing community, to help them to plan their trainings and to control their results which they can share with other members.
Sugarrays positions itself as a club that doesn't impose you boxercising but a place where you learn boxing and build your confidence to be ready for sparrings. Outdoor advertisings work as call for action and communicate this idea to the target audience.
One of the most important phase of my work is brainstorming for design concepts and solutions to better communicate the message through visuals.
