lavazza

Logo Design. Brand Identity. Package Design.
Web Design. Advertising.

For more than 100 years Lavazza has been bringing delicious coffee to Europe and now wants to expand its business into Canadian market. There is no better time for the new brand design. Simple, fresh and consistent design reflects perfectly the company's ability to combine traditions with modern innovations. Natural, light colour scheme was selected to highlight the environmentally friendly and socially responsible business direction. The inspiration for the advertising campaign came from the company's passion for art and creativity and their strong connection with their customers. The new design is up-to-date, pleasant and elegant with a little twist in every aspect the same as the flavour and aroma of Lavazza coffee.
