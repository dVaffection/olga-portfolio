Hi, I'm Olga. Sometimes though you can find my name spelled Volha. Yes, it's the same name, just on Belarussain.

I'm from Belarus and we speak two languages there: Russain and Belarussain. I also learnt French in school and improved it in the university while getting my diploma in translation and interpretation. Translation is fun. I enjoyed working in the industry for 3 years. And then I moved to Vancouver, Canada. I liked it and decided to stay.

It was 5 years ago. And here I learnt English and also discovered the language of design which I love ever since. I got my education in Visual Colledge of Art and Design of Vancouver and have been working as freelance and in-house graphic designer for the last 3 years, designing both for print and web.

I have worked on a variety of different print and digital projects for small and large companies. I’m proud to deliver logo designs, brand identities, promotional and marketing materials, web and ui/ux designs to help companies to stand out among competitors, to build awareness on the market and to create strong relationships with clients through experience that reinforces commercial and emotional value of the company.

I’m an advocate for a beautiful, clean, functional and appropriate design. Design is all around us and I want people be surrounded by positive emotions and pleasant things.
